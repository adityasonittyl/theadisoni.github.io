About AWS Community Day 2026

AWS Community Day Rajasthan 2026 is an immersive community-driven event built around the theme “Building AWSome — Cloud Clubs × Professionals.” It brings together developers, cloud practitioners, DevOps and SRE professionals, students, and open-source enthusiasts at one place.

The event features interactive sessions, quizzes, contests, and more, creating an engaging learning experience. It offers a valuable opportunity for budding learners and tech enthusiasts to gain insights into modern technology stacks, along with industry-level exposure through hands-on sessions led by experienced professionals.

FAQ

  • What is AWS UG Jaipur - Rajasthan, how can I join?

    The Official Community Group of AWS & Cloud Computing Enthusiasts, Passionate Developers, Architects, Users & Evangelists and Students in and around Rajasthan, India.

    Join us on Meetup

  • Why you should join us?

    This is a platform where you can Interact with Highly qualified and experienced industry professionals and like-minded people, here you get plenty of opportunities to learn, grow, share with others, you will be getting access to various opensource, DevOps, CloudNative, SDEs, AWS Global Events, AWS Training and Certifications, AWS learning resources, and whatnot, This also gives you a bright chance to win Goodies, Swags, and AWS Credits during events and sessions.

  • By who and to whom?

    AWS Community Day Rajasthan 2026 is organized by the AWS User Group Jaipur – Rajasthan, in collaboration with AWS Cloud Clubs from across the region.

    AWS Cloud Clubs represent student-led AWS learning communities from colleges, working alongside professionals to strengthen the broader AWS ecosystem.

    The event focuses on educating the community about AWS solutions in cloud, DevOps, AI, ML, and open-source technologies, helping participants develop skills to design, deploy, and operate modern infrastructure and applications. It is open to developers, students, and industry enthusiasts through interactive sessions, keynote talks, and hands-on learning experiences.

  • What are the perks available for the participants?

    Interact with highly qualified and experienced industry professionals and like-minded people, and a chance to win Goodies, Swags, and amazon vouchers or AWS Credits during the conference via contests or quizzes.
