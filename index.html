About Us

The Official Community Group of AWS & Cloud Computing Enthusiasts, Passionate Developers, Architects, Users & Evangelists and Students in and around Rajasthan - India. Here we organize free talks to educate you about the AWS Products, Services, and Solutions in the Cloud and help you develop the skills to Design, Deploy, and Operate Infrastructure and Applications. This Group allows users to ask questions, get answers, share ideas, and learn about the latest updates.
